Nothing Is What It Seems on Convict Island

Jhalon enters his senior year of high school with two simple goals:

  • Get the girl
  • Get into college

But those goals change once he confesses to a crime he didn't commit to keep his brother safe. After he's convicted as an adult, he finds himself in prison surrounded by grown men. Just when he doesn't think things can get any worse, he's shipped to a secret government-owned island run by convicts.

Now, surrounded by adult murderers, Jhalon has two new goals:

  • Get to know his enemy
  • Get off the island

To do that, he'll have to figure out who to trust...and who wants to bury him alive!
