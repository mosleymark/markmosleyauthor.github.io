About Mark Mosley

Mark Mosley is a proud Ravenclaw, vintage video gamer (yeah Battletoads!), and Marvel nerd who somehow developed an insane passion for teaching Language Arts to teenagers.

After spending so many years working with high school students (some of whom hadn't voluntarily read a book since Dr. Seuss), Mark set out on a mission: write a story that even the most reluctant readers wouldn't want to put down.

The result was Convict Island, a fast-paced young adult thriller packed with mystery, sarcasm, humor, and action.

When he's not writing or teaching, Mark can usually be found gaming, fly fishing, or driving one of his 4 children somewhere.
